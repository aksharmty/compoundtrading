💰 SIP Trading Calculator

This calculator helps you estimate how your monthly investment (SIP) can grow using a compounding trading strategy. It considers your monthly contribution, trading frequency, profit percentage, and trading fees to project how long it may take to reach your target fund.

Disclaimer:
This calculator provides an estimated projection based on fixed inputs such as profit rate and fees. Actual trading results may vary due to market conditions, slippage, execution delays, and risk factors. This is not financial advice. Please trade responsibly and use proper risk management.

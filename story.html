My Trading Journey – From 500 USDT to a Big Win

By Raj Kumar | Educational Purpose Only

It all started with a small test fund of just 500 USDT. I had always been curious about how automated trading really works — not the hype, but the science behind it. I wanted to understand how algorithms can take emotions out of trading and replace them with logic, precision, and consistency.

Step 1: Understanding the Logic

Before writing a single line of code, I studied how exchange APIs communicate. I learned about REST API calls, order books, and how every candle represents a story of market psychology. It was fascinating to see how math and emotion intersect in markets.

Step 2: Building My First Bot

I chose HitBTC because it provides a stable and developer-friendly API. Using PHP, I coded my own bot — starting from fetching live data, then placing test trades, and finally optimizing my algorithm for compounding growth.

Step 3: The Power of Compounding

My focus was never on getting rich overnight. I built the bot to simulate small but consistent growth — using compounding principles to multiply over time. Even a few percent gain per week can lead to exponential growth when managed correctly.

Step 4: Lessons Learned

  • Trading is 90% psychology, 10% execution.
  • Automating emotions is the first step toward discipline.
  • Every strategy must be tested — not believed.
  • Consistency beats intensity, every single time.
